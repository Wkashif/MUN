Model United Nations is more than a conference; it is a platform that nurtures leadership, critical thinking, diplomacy, and responsible global citizenship. Through meaningful debate and collaboration, students learn to analyze global challenges, respect diverse perspectives, and articulate their ideas with confidence and integrity. At Pakistan School Muscat, we strongly believe in providing opportunities that extend learning beyond the classroom. PSM MUN reflects our commitment to empowering students with the skills and values needed to succeed in an increasingly interconnected world. I encourage all delegates to participate wholeheartedly, challenge themselves, and embrace this experience with curiosity and purpose. May this journey inspire you to lead with knowledge, empathy, and responsibility—Chairman of The Board of Directors
