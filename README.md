<h1>
  PSM MUN website 
</h1>
<p>
 I created a website for our school’s Model United Nations (MUN) team because it was still new and didn’t really have clear guidelines or a proper structure. I thought it would be helpful to have everything in one place so new delegates could easily understand how MUN works and what they need to do.

The website includes pages like Home, Guidelines, Registration, Gallery, and Feedback. The guidelines section helps beginners learn the basics, the registration page makes signing up easier, and the gallery shows highlights from events. I also added a feedback section so participants can share their experience and help improve future sessions.
There is also a teams page however their management team was never finalised thus dummy pictures are in place.

</p>
